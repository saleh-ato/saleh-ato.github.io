Saleh Toodarvari

Web Developer and Graphic Designer

Saleh Toodarvari is a website designer, photographer, and graphic artist who brings together technical skill and artistic creativity. Born in Tehran in July 2000, he earned a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering from Damghan University, a foundation that supports his thoughtful and innovative approach to digital design.

Saleh’s diverse skill set is reflected in his experience, from building responsive websites using Python’s Flask framework, Bootstrap, JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3, to creating engaging graphic designs with tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Lightroom. He also focuses on practical aspects of web development, including server administration and SEO strategies, ensuring that the digital experiences he designs are both visually appealing and highly functional.

Professional Experience

Demonstrated excellence in roles such as Website Designer and Support at Fidar Ghete Pasargad, Graphic Designer at Hafez Printing, and Web Designer & Junior DevOps Engineer at Abanet.
